Gallery

The Smiling Moon

Description

This art piece is about an old man standing in the wildernis. The old man waves at the moon and the moon is happy because he waves at him. This art piece is about the connection between humans and nature, and how even in solitude, we can find joy and companionship in the world around us.

It Lurks

Description

This art piece shows Zebra standing on a farm. He is watched by a lot of creepy beings and he doesn't realize it. This art piece shows that danger is always lurking and that always you need to be careful. Even when everytings seems good.

At My Limit

Description

This art piece shows a picture of an old man who is tired. He is restless and has worked a lot. This shows that even when your old, life can be hard sometimes. It is hard to find rest in this time full of incentives and distractions. No matter your age.

Beyond Labels

Description

This art piece shows my vision of pigeon holding. You see a lot of different flowers. Each one represents a person. And you see one flower that stands out from the rest. He is different yet he is the happiest of them all. This reminds us that being different isn't something to hide, it's something to celebrate.

About

Hi! My name is Ruben and I am from the Netherlands. I create paintings using acrylic paint and enjoy working with bright colors and striking combinations. Painting and drawing is my way of being creative and bringing my ideas to life. I enjoy making art that radiates energy and makes people happy. I dont have a specific art style because i like to experiment. Every painting is made with care and passion, making each piece unique. Thank you for taking a look at my website. I hope you enjoy my art and perhaps find a painting that suits you.
